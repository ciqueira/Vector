# Vector Legal Documents

[English](README.md) · [Português](pt-BR/README.md)

Last updated: July 17, 2026

These documents apply specifically to Vector and its optional
Supporter purchase. MCNexus has separate documents for the distribution,
installation, activation, update, and platform-security services it provides.

The stable published product pages used by Commerce are available at
[legal.magnociqueira.com.br/products/vector/](https://legal.magnociqueira.com.br/products/vector/).
The Markdown files in this repository remain available for discoverability and
review; the version recorded with a purchase identifies the terms accepted for
that order.

```text
Vector (product controller and seller)
├── TERMS.md                 product and Supporter terms
├── PRIVACY.md               product-level personal-data processing
├── REFUND_POLICY.md         cancellation and refund rules
└── SUPPORT_POLICY.md        scope of priority support

MCNexus (distribution and licensing platform)
├── TERMS.md                 platform rules and responsibilities
└── PRIVACY.md               platform and infrastructure processing

Third-party providers
├── GitHub                   identity authentication
├── Stripe                   checkout and payment processing
├── OpenKey / Cryptlex       license management, as configured
└── MailerLite               operational email delivery, as configured
```

The product documents do not replace the Nexus documents. Both layers may
apply to the same transaction because one governs the product and commercial
benefit while the other governs the delivery and licensing infrastructure.
