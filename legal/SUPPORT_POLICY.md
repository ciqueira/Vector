# Vector Support Policy

[English](SUPPORT_POLICY.md) · [Português](pt-BR/SUPPORT_POLICY.md)

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Document version: `vector-support-2026-07-17`

## 1. Free community support

All Vector users may consult public documentation and report
reproducible product bugs through the repository's public issue forms. Public
support is best-effort and has no guaranteed response time.

Do not include a complete license key, payment data, private project files, or
personal data in a public issue.

## 2. Priority Supporter support

An active Vector Supporter benefit includes private priority email
support for 12 months after confirmed payment. The support email must match the
eligible license or be reasonably verified as authorized by its holder.

Priority means that valid Supporter requests are triaged before ordinary
best-effort questions. The initial response target is five Brazilian business
days. This is a service target, not a guaranteed resolution deadline or a
24-hour emergency service.

Support is provided remotely in English or Portuguese. The response target is
based on Brazilian business days and does not follow the buyer's local time
zone or public holidays.

## 3. Included scope

Priority support covers reasonable assistance with:

- installation, activation, deactivation, and license recovery;
- supported host, operating-system, Metal, and CUDA compatibility;
- reproducing Vector defects;
- identifying whether an issue belongs to the plugin, MCNexus, the host, the
  operating system, or the GPU environment; and
- purchase, Supporter status, refunds, and operational email delivery.

Support may request diagnostic information such as versions, operating system,
GPU model, host application, steps to reproduce, and redacted logs.

## 4. Exclusions

Support does not include custom feature development, guaranteed implementation
of requests, color-grading or production consulting, host-vendor support,
recovery of damaged project files, remote workstation administration, or
guaranteed compatibility with unsupported systems and hardware.

Abusive, unlawful, automated, or excessive requests may be limited after
reasonable notice. Mandatory consumer remedies and good-faith security reports
remain unaffected.
