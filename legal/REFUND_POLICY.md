## 1. How to request cancellation or a refund

Use the [electronic cancellation form](https://legal.magnociqueira.com.br/cancel/)
or send a private request to
[tools@magnociqueira.com.br](mailto:tools@magnociqueira.com.br) with:

- the email used at checkout;
- the approximate payment date and amount;
- the Stripe receipt or Checkout reference, if available; and
- a short description of the request.

Do not publish payment details or license keys in GitHub Issues. Additional
information may be requested only when reasonably needed to verify the payer
and prevent fraud.

## 2. Mandatory withdrawal and remedies

For a Brazilian consumer distance transaction, a timely request to exercise the
right of withdrawal will be handled in accordance with Article 49 of the
Brazilian Consumer Protection Code and the E-commerce Decree, including the
applicable seven-day period. More favorable mandatory law in the consumer's
jurisdiction remains applicable.

For buyers outside Brazil, no voluntary refund is offered solely because of a
change of preference or non-use. Any mandatory withdrawal, cancellation,
warranty, or refund right in the consumer's country or region remains fully
preserved. Payment in a foreign currency does not waive those rights.

A refund or correction will also be assessed when required by law, including
duplicate charges, an unauthorized or fraudulent payment, failure to provide
the purchased benefit, or a defect that cannot be remedied within the legally
applicable process.

## 3. Requests after mandatory periods

After mandatory withdrawal periods and remedies have expired, purchases are
normally final. No voluntary refund is promised solely because the buyer
changed preference, did not use support, expected an unpromised feature, or no
longer needs the plugin.

This rule does not exclude mandatory consumer rights and does not prevent the
seller from approving an exceptional refund after reviewing the facts.

## 4. Effect of a refund

When a payment is refunded, the paid Supporter period and related priority
support may end. The separate free Vector license is not automatically
revoked merely because a Supporter payment was refunded, unless independent
license abuse or another lawful ground exists.

Refund timing after approval depends on Stripe, the payment method, and the
buyer's financial institution. Any currency conversion or issuer processing is
controlled by those providers.

## 5. Disputes and chargebacks

Contacting support first usually allows a faster review. A chargeback or dispute
may cause the paid benefit to be marked as under review while Stripe and the
financial institutions investigate. It does not eliminate mandatory rights or
authorize retaliation against an unrelated free license.
